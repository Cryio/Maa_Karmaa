About

We are pleased to introduce MAA KARMAA GLOBAL ENGINEERING LLP. a company comprising dynamic and experienced engineers specializing in construction and equipment technology. Our company operates in various fields of civil and mechanical construction. Our parent company is KARMA TECH.

We are the pioneer company in INDIA to use advance slipform Technology for fast and safe construction of Chimney, Silo and high rise structures. We are leading manufacturer and exporter of Heavy lifting equipments (12MT, 18MT & 25MT) and slip-form equipments.

Director of company having 24 years of experience in Chimney and silo field, including some projects with Reliance industries ltd., Manglore refinery petrochemicals ltd, Essar, Adani power, Ashoka buildcon ltd. and many more.

MKGEL has added to its achievements some of the modern and innovative buildings construction in various location of INDIA. We provide contracting services to any kind of industrial construction and undertake commercial and residential ventures as well. We have worked in various industrial projects; cement factories, float glass factories, power plants, pharmaceutical, glassware and many more. We have a vast experience of large scale project handling.
